Rules of Padel

  • The game is played in doubles.
  • The ball must bounce on the ground before hitting the walls.
  • Serves must be underhand and bounce in the opponent's service box.
  • Points are scored when the opponent fails to return the ball correctly.
  • The scoring system is the same as tennis: 15, 30, 40, and game.
